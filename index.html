Homemade Meatball

Recipe source: iheartnaptime.com

Ingredients

  • Ground beef – 500 g (1 lb)
  • Breadcrumbs – 1 cup
  • Milk – 1/4 cup
  • Egg – 1 large
  • Grated Parmesan cheese – 1/4 cup
  • Garlic – 2 cloves, minced
  • Salt – 1 tsp
  • Black pepper – 1/2 tsp
  • Italian seasoning – 1 tsp
  • Olive oil – 2 tbsp (for frying)
  • Tomato sauce – 2 cups (for simmering)

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Baking sheet or skillet
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Wooden spoon or spatula
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Oven or stove
  • Small bowl for sauce

Directions

  1. Prepare the meatball mixture: In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, Parmesan, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, and Italian seasoning. Mix until just combined.
  2. Form the meatballs: Using your hands, shape the mixture into 1- to 1.5-inch meatballs and place them on a baking sheet or plate.
  3. Cook the meatballs: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Brown the meatballs on all sides for 5–7 minutes, then add tomato sauce and simmer for 15–20 minutes until fully cooked.
  4. Serve: Arrange the meatballs on a plate and spoon some of the sauce over them. Optionally, garnish with fresh parsley or extra Parmesan.
