Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Measuring cups
  • Electric mixer or spoon
  • Baking sheet
  • Oven

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and baking powder.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla.
  5. Gradually blend in dry ingredients.
  6. Roll dough into balls and place on baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 8–10 minutes until lightly golden.
